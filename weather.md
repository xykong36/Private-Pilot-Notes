A: [Aviation Weather - GFA Tool](https://www.aviationweather.gov/gfa) 不断拉近距离 可以用来看Clouds / 



Condensed METAR Board

- **CIG** - Ceiling
- **VIS** - Visibility
- **WX** - Weather (thunderstorms, freezing rain)
- **WSpd** - Wind speed
- **WGst** - Wind gusts
